Unit 1

What's your favourite season?

Janet:Look at all the colourful flowers and the beautiful birds in the trees.I really love spring.What's your favourite season, Ben?
Ben:Summer.I love swimming and summer is the best time for swimming.Do you like summer, Jiamin?
Jiamin:I like swimming but I don't like summer.It is too hot.I prefer winter.When it snows, I can make a snowman.
Xiaoling:I don't like summer and I don't like winter, either.My favourite season is autumn.It's very windy and I can fly a kite.

Unit 2

It's the middle of winter

Ben:Hi, everyone!This is my cousin Reg.He is visiting me for the summer holidays.
Xiaoling:I don't understand.It is January now, the middle of winter.How can you have a summer holiday?
Reg:I'm from Australia.Our summer is from December to February, so it is summer in Australia now.
Jiamin:I see now.When it is summer in Australia, it is winter in China.
Xiaoling:What's summer like in Australia, Reg?
Reg:It's very hot, so we always go swimming.
Ben:It's too cold to swim now.But if you are here from July to August, we can go swimming every day.

Unit 3

We are going to have an English test

Janet:What's the date, Ben?
Ben:The first of June.Why?
Janet:We are going to see a film tomorrow evening.Don't forget.
Ben:Isn't that Mike's birthday?We are all going to his home for a party tomorrow.
Janet:No.Mike's birthday party is on June 11th.
Ben:Is there anything else important this month?
Janet:Yes.We are going to have an English test on June 24th.Don't forget.

Unit 4

Have a good time in Hainan

Next week we will travel in Hainan for five days.We will fly there early in the morning on Tuesday, November 23rd.In the afternoon, we will go for a picnic.On the 24th we will go mountain climbing all day.November 25th is a free day.We can go shopping, swim in the pool or play on the beach.On the 26th we are going to visit a farm.I can't wait to see all the lovely deer.That evening we will camp under the stars.Then we will fly back home at noon the next day.I'm sure we'll have a good time in Hainan.

Unit 5

Would you like to go with us?

Janet:Hey, guys.What are you doing this Saturday?
Ben:Nothing.Why?
Janet:My cousin and I plan to go on a picnic.Would you like to go with us?
Jiamin:A picnic?That sounds great!
Ben:What time should we go?
Janet:Let's meet at the school gate at 9 a.m.We can all go to the park together.
Ben:Cool.The picnic is this Saturday, August 14th, right?
Jiamin:Oh dear.I'm sorry but I am going to my grandma's birthday party that day.
Janet:No problem.Maybe next time.

Unit 7

We will go by train

Janet:Do you have any plans for the holiday?
Ben:Yes.I will go travelling with my family.We are going to Shenzhen.
Xiaoling:How will you go there?
Ben:We will go by train.We will leave on October 2nd.I can't wait.
Janet:It's very exciting.What will you do in Shenzhen?
Ben:Well...we are going to visit Window of the World, of course.And I want to go to Happy Valley, too.
Janet:When will you come back home?
Ben:We will come back home on October 4th.

Unit 8

Ben's first trip to Beijing

It's Ben's first trip to Beijing and he is very excited.He came by plane this morning.Now he is going to Tian'anmen.It is near his hotel so he can go there on foot.This evening Ben and his Beijing friend Li Hua will have dinner together.Tomorrow morning, they will drive to the Great Wall.Ben wants to take some photos there.In the afternoon they are going to the Summer Palace.The palace is big and beautiful.They are going to take a walk around the lake.On Friday morning, Ben will buy some Chinese clothes in Wangfujing Street for his parents.He will go there by underground.Ben will fly back home on Saturday.

Unit 9

Be careful

Mrs Webb:Come on, kids.It's time to cook the noodles.
Ben:Oops!
Mrs Webb:Be careful, Ben.Don't run near the hot water.You'll get hurt.Cooking can be fun, but you must be careful.
Ben:Yes.You're right.
Xiaoling:Don't cut the vegetables so quickly, Ben.The knife is sharp.You may cut yourself.
Mrs Webb:Watch out, everyone.The floor is very wet.Don't walk there.You may fall down.
Ben:Here...Let me help you dry the floor.
Mrs Webb:Great work, Ben.Safety first.Don't forget.

Unit 10

How to stay safe

Children's Safety Dos and Don'ts

Do cross the street at the traffic lights or safety crossing.Don't cross the road when the traffic light is red.Don't climb tall trees.Don't run on the stairs.Don't play in the street.Don't take any money or candy from strangers in the street.Do tell your teacher if you see a classmate in trouble.Don't leave the school during school hours without asking your teacher.

Unit 11

Can you tell me the way?

Janet:Oh dear!I think we're lost.
Ben:Let's go and ask the lady over there.
Janet:Excuse me.Can you tell me the way to the hospital, please?
Lady:Of course.Let me see.Go straight ahead then turn right at the second crossing.
Ben:Is the hospital on the left or on the right?
Lady:It's the building on your right, opposite the school.You can't miss it.
Janet and Ben:Thank you so much.

Unit 12

I know a short cut

John and Joan were standing in front of their house and John looked at his watch.
John:We'll be late."The film starts in ten minutes, but we need 15 minutes to get there," John said to Joan.
He started to walk quickly.
Joan:Don't worry."We have time," Joan said.I know a short cut.
To get to the cinema, John usually turns left out of their house.Then he takes the first right, goes straight ahead and takes the second left.But today Joan took him a different way.They crossed the main street and turned left into the park.They walked through the park and out of the front gate.Suddenly they were in front of the cinema.John looked at his watch.
John:I can't believe it.We are on time.You're really great, Joan!
