Once Upon a Time

Page 1

A Young Wizard

Once upon a time there was a young wizard.
His name was Tom.
Tom studied hard at wizard school.
But he was not good at magic.

Page 2

A Tree, A Bee

“Tom, turn me into a tree,” said Master Wizard.
Tom waved his wand.
Poof!
Master Wizard turned into a bee!
The other wizards laughed.

Page 3

Why Be A Wizard?

Master Wizard sighed.
“Tell me again, Tom. Why do you want to be a wizard?”

Page 4

A Fine Wish

“I want to use magic to help people,” said Tom.
“That’s a fine wish, Tom,” said Master.
“But you must practice—a lot!”

Page 5

A Messenger

One day a trumpet blared.
It was a messenger from the palace!

Page 6

The King Needs A Wizard

“Hear this!” cried the messenger.
“The king needs a new royal wizard. This school may send one student to try out.”

Page 7

Tom Has An Idea

“Maybe I can try out,” thought Tom.
“I could use magic to help the whole kingdom!”
Later Tom found Master Wizard.

Page 8

May I Try Out?

“Master, may I try out?” Tom asked.
The other wizards laughed.
“You can’t be the royal wizard,” said Walter.
“You’re no good at magic!”

Page 9

We’ll See

“We’ll see, Tom,” Master said kindly.
“Hooray!” Tom cheered.
He waved his wand by mistake.
Poof!
“You fool!” Walter cried.

Page 10

Oops!

Tom looked up.
The ceiling had a big hole.
“Oops!” said Tom.
